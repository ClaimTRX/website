Unlocking Growth: Project Staking with the CFT Ecosystem VC Fund

The CFT Ecosystem is committed to fostering innovation and supporting promising blockchain projects. One of our key initiatives, the Project Staking Program, serves as an initial Venture Capital (VC) initiative designed to provide funding and financial sustainability for projects within the ecosystem.

What is Project Staking?

Project Staking is a strategic funding mechanism that allows projects to raise 50,000 CFT through community contributions and personal investments. Once this threshold is reached, the CFT contract locks for six months, and the project begins receiving income from the ecosystem. This income is funded by a 5% allocation from the total revenue generated by the CFT Ecosystem.

At the end of six months, the contract unlocks, allowing all contributors to withdraw their CFT back while the project benefits from the financial backing and support.

How Does it Work?

  • A Project Applies for Staking: Projects submit a request to participate in the Project Staking Program.
  • Community and Project Contributions: The project’s team and community raise CFT tokens and stake them in a dedicated smart contract.
  • Reaching 50,000 CFT: Once the staking contract reaches 50,000 CFT, it locks for six months, ensuring financial stability for the project.
  • Project Receives Income: The CFT ecosystem distributes 5% of all generated income to support the staked projects.
  • Unlock & Withdrawal: After six months, the contract opens, and contributors can withdraw their staked CFT, marking the end of the staking income.

Additional Funding Through the CFT VC Fund

Some projects may struggle to raise the full 50,000 CFT. To support such initiatives, the CFT VC Fund provides supplementary funding before the contract locks.

For example, if a project raises 10,000 CFT from its community and 20,000 CFT from its own reserves, it can apply for the CFT VC Fund to cover the remaining 20,000 CFT. Once the total reaches 50,000 CFT, the contract locks, and the project begins receiving income as part of the program.

Why Participate in Project Staking?

  • Financial Stability: Projects receive sustainable income from the CFT ecosystem, allowing them to focus on growth and development.
  • Community Engagement: Engaging with the CFT community builds trust and long-term supporters for the project.
  • No Risk for Stakers: Contributors can reclaim their staked CFT after six months, making it a secure way to support projects.
  • Access to Additional Funding: The CFT VC Fund helps projects reach the required threshold for staking, ensuring that promising ideas do not fail due to funding gaps.

Join the Project Staking Program

Are you a project looking for funding and sustainable income? The CFT Project Staking Program offers a unique opportunity to secure financial support while aligning with a growing ecosystem.

Apply today and become part of a decentralized financial future where innovation thrives!
