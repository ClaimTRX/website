CFT Token Evolution: V1 to V2 Migration and Ecosystem Updates 🔥

Dear Amazing CFT Community, we’re thrilled to share that our CFT token sale is complete, and we’re ready to embark on the next phase of our journey! Here’s everything you need to know about the transition to CFT V2 and exciting updates to the CFT Ecosystem.

1. CFT Token Migration: From V1 to V2

To enhance trading capabilities and support future growth, the CFT token is upgrading from V1 to V2. All CFT holders are required to migrate their tokens to the new V2 contract via our official website.

  • Same Supply, Name, and Logo: CFT V2 maintains the same 9,800,000 token supply, name, and logo as V1.
  • New Contract Address: The V2 token operates on a new smart contract to enable advanced functionality.
  • Action Required: Visit our migration page to complete the migration process.

2. Staking Contract Updates

To align with the V2 transition, some staking contracts will require action by April 23, 2025:

  • Contracts Requiring Unstaking and Restaking:
    • CFT with CFT rewards
    • KING with CFT rewards
    • FYM with CFT rewards
    • StableX with CFT rewards

    Holders must unstake from the old contracts and restake in the new V2 contracts. Rewards from old contracts will cease on April 23, 2025.

  • Contracts Ending on April 23, 2025:
    • CFT with TRX rewards
    • CFT with USDT rewards
    • HOS with CFT rewards

    These contracts will not transition to V2 and will stop paying rewards on the specified date.

3. Energy Requirements for Ecosystem Interactions

All interactions within the CFT Ecosystem, including token migration and staking, require energy. We encourage our community to support each other by sharing energy where possible.

  • Our Commitment: We’ll do our best to provide energy for migration to those in need, but please note that assistance may be limited due to high demand.
  • Community Strength: Let’s work together to ensure a smooth transition for everyone!

4. What’s Next for CFT?

Year one was about laying a strong foundation—Year two is all about expansion! Stay tuned for exciting announcements, including:

  • New staking pools
  • Strategic partnerships
  • Enhanced ecosystem features

We’re committed to building a decentralized future where innovation thrives, and we couldn’t do it without you!
